# GeoSelect.it Brand Guidelines

## Logo Usage
- Use official vector logo (SVG preferred) provided in `/branding/logo/`
- Maintain sufficient clearspace: minimum half logo height on all sides.
- Never distort, recolor, or place the logo on visually busy backgrounds.
- Use “Powered by GeoSelect.it” attribution logo in partner surfaces and SaaS white label footers.

## Brand Voice
- Friendly, concise, and accessible. Focus on clear communication and helpful tone.
- Avoid excessive jargon—GeoSelect.it is approachable for users at any tech skill level.

## Iconography
- Use simple, line or duotone icons matching the style of your logo.
- Consistent icon sizing (16px, 24px).

## Accessibility Guide
- Maintain contrast ratios as in your `/branding/colors.md`.
- All text above 16px for body, 22px+ for mobile headers.
- All interactive elements (buttons, links, inputs) must have a visible :focus state.

## Powered By
- Place “Powered by GeoSelect.it” in app footers, reports, or key onboarding screens.
