# Happy HOA Color Palette

## Primary Colors

### Primary Blue
- **Name:** Ocean Blue
- **Hex:** `#2563EB`
- **RGB:** `rgb(37, 99, 235)`
- **HSL:** `hsl(217, 92%, 53%)`
- **Usage:** Primary buttons, headers, active states, brand identity

### Secondary Blue
- **Name:** Sky Blue
- **Hex:** `#3B82F6`
- **RGB:** `rgb(59, 130, 246)`
- **HSL:** `hsl(217, 97%, 60%)`
- **Usage:** Secondary buttons, hover states, highlights

### Accent Yellow
- **Name:** Sunshine Gold
- **Hex:** `#FCD34D`
- **RGB:** `rgb(252, 211, 77)`
- **HSL:** `hsl(48, 97%, 65%)`
- **Usage:** Accent elements, alerts, success indicators, badges

## Neutral Colors

- **Hex:** `#F0F4F8`
- **RGB:** `rgb(240, 244, 248)`
- **HSL:** `hsl(214, 32%, 97%)`
- **Usage:** Page backgrounds, card backgrounds, light UI elements

### Text Dark

- **RGB:** `rgb(31, 41, 55)`

### Text Light

- **HSL:** `hsl(218, 11%, 45%)`
- **Usage:** Secondary text, labels, helper text

### Border

## Color Combinations

### Recommended Pairings
- **Primary with Accent:** Ocean Blue (#2563EB) + Sunshine Gold (#FCD34D)
- **Text on Light:** Charcoal (#1F2937) on Ghost White (#F0F4F8)
- **Text on Dark:** Ghost White (#F0F4F8) on Charcoal (#1F2937)
- **Hover States:** Sky Blue (#3B82F6) (lighter than primary)

### Accessibility
- All color combinations meet WCAG AA standards for contrast ratios
- Primary text contrast ratio: 10.5:1 (minimum 4.5:1)
- Non-text element contrast ratio: 5.1:1 (minimum 3:1)
